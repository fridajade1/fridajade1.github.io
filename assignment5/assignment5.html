Sun and The Moon Poem

The sun and moon, a perfect pair, Beautiful opposites shining bright, so rare,

The sun with its fiery, brilliant glow, The moon with its ethereal, gentle flow. One rises in the east, the other in the west,

Two celestial beings in a cosmic duet, The sun's warmth embraces the earth below,

While the moon's light shimmers with a mystical glow. The sun burns with an intense fervor,

While the moon's soft light soothes and purrs, One blazes with energy, the other serene,

Together they paint a picture, a cosmic scene. The sun and moon, a yin and yang,

Two sides of the same celestial gang, Opposites that balance each other,Keeping the cosmos in perfect order.So let us marvel at their contrast and harmony,

As the sun sinks low and the moon rises calmly, Admiring the beauty in their duality, The perfect balance of this celestial reality.
